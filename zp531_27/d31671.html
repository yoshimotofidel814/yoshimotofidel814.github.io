【本の内容】
Office design has experienced dramatic changes in recent years. Technological advances have led to new ways of working and the design of work spaces has consequently adapted. This volume presents a selection of projects exemplifying the transformation of spaces into workplaces. Illustrated with plans images and text these projects revise ideas related to the efficient use of space allowing for new needs the change in use of rooms creation and use of communal areas dual use working and living spaces and more. Original and inventive they demonstrate that solutions can often be found by ignoring established rules.
