Guitar Luthier Repair Tool Kit:

a set of simple and practical guitar repair tool kit, suitable for guitar, bass, mandolin, banjo and more. Perfect gift idea for guitar lovers, beginners, luthiers.

Guitar Fret Leveling Sanding Beam (6 inch): 

made of aluminum alloy material, strong, rust-proof and fine workmanship. There are four kinds of self-adhesive sandpapers (400#, 600#, 1000#, 1200#) on four sides of the level beam, applied to meet your different polishing needs.

Guitar Fret Crowning Luthier File:

Guitar String Action Ruler Gauge:

made of stainless steel & pocket-sized. Used to measure string height, bridge saddle height, pickup polepiece height, nut height, saddle slot depth, fret slot depth, etc. Also printed with a conversion chart of string action at 12th fret.
