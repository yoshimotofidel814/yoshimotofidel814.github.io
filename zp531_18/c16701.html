商品コメント This magnetic mount can be used to stick Action 2 to smooth, flat surfaces. The adhesive base and ball-joint design allow you to stick Action 2 to surfaces at different angles to gain unique vantage points.
Remove the adhesive base, and attach tripods, extension rods, or other third-party accessories via the 1/4" screw hole.
The nano-glue base is reusable after washing, making it easy to fix the camera at hand and provide more creative shooting perspectives.
Do not stick the nano adhesive base to surfaces with oil, water, dust, dirt or rough particles (such as walls with powder and peeling, or floors with dust and gravel) to avoid falling off due to poor adhesion.
When used under strenuous movement scenes, it is recommended to use with magnetic adapter, quick release connector and curved adhesive base.
