説明: Our story

We are a small family business jewelry brand which started from 1990s.
5 years ago, we are nearly destroyed by cheaply made fast fashion e-commerce industry.
We learned from the pain,decided to optimize our sales channels by opening e-commerce shops in Us,Uk,Europe,Japan,update product designs by hiring 40+ new designers from famous brands ,and cut unnecessary marketing costs.

We have never thought of giving up the purely hand-made production method, although machine production can greatly improve efficiency and reduce costs.
No doubt that this is a road full of thorns, but we decided to stick to it.

Thus We Rebirthed from the ashes.

Now Halukakah has become an influential jewelry brand.We proudly served 100000+ satisfied customers per year.
If you straighten the necklaces and bracelets we sold every year,you will find that their total length can circle around the earth.

3 Visions of Halukakah

First,Fashion is not about let kings enjoy the best jewels, but about making them affordable for everyone.
Having our own factories based in East Asia,which makes it possible for everyone to enjoy top quality design jewelries at a reasonable price.

The second,Bring business back to its original state.Let customers pay for the product itself.
Halukakah aim to define the standards of jewelry making.

The third,The world filled with shoddy machines has squeezed the living space of real craftsmen handed down from generation to generation.
We strive to make it possible for these precious traditional craftsmanship can be passed on ,and help craftsmen live a decent life.

Future Plans

Our goal is to serve 1000000+ satisfied customers per year,and establish the Halukakah Family Association for our best friends,loyal customers supporting us.
At Halukakah, our aim isn"t to sell you jewelry,is build a relationship.
