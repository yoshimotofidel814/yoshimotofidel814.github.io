The result is this TYGA Performance GP-T style silencer. Available in three models、 with carbon、 carbon／Kevlar and aluminium outer tubes and with stainless end caps、 it is mounted by two springs (included) for ease of installation and for looks、 will fit on 1 1／8 inch tube.

The silencer is strong and easily rebuildable、 using rivets to assemble. Remember to order two silencers for every twin cylinder exhaust system！ Also see EXSL-0069 and EXSL-0069.

Note、 this silencer is only compatible with the NX5 RS250R exhaust system and cannot be mounted on other exhaust chambers in our product range.
