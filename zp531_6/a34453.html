Each new print copy includes Navigate 2 Advantage Access that unlocks a comprehensive and interactive eBook animations student practice activities and assessments a full suite of instructor resources and learning analytics reporting tools.Written with non-majors in mind Discovering Nutrition Sixth Edition introduces students to the fundamentals of nutrition with an engaging and personalized approach.
