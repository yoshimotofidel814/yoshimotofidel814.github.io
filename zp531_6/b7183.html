説明:
BOYA BY-LM40 is an ultracompact clip-on lavalier microphone with a standard USB-A connector that plugs directly into Windows PCs and Apple Mac computers to capture clear, broadcast-quality sound.

It offers easy plug-and-play operation, and the 13-foot (4m) built-in cable provides a comfortable amount of length to plug into the computer in front of you.

It features an omnidirectional pickup pattern that captures sound equally in every direction, which means you don’t need to worry about which direction it’s pointing in when you clip it to your shirt. Every tool to get the job done. The included alligator-style mic clip firmly holds the mic in place when attached to your shirt. A foam windscreen is also included, which diffuses distracting wind noise. A reusable cable strap keeps the wire tidy, and a lined storage pouch with a locking drawstring holds everything neatly when not in use. You get all the tools you need to capture excellent sound quality in any situation, with a package that fits in your pocket.
